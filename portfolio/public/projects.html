Wordle

Description

Wordle clone based on the NYtimes wordle game

How to play:

The player has 6 guess to guess a random 5 letter word selected from a pool of words.
After each guess the letters will change colors to either green, yellow or gray.
Green means the letter is in the correct position in the word.
Yellow means the letter exists in the word but not in the correct position.
Gray means the letter is not in the word.
The player wins if the word is guessed and loses if the word is not guessed after 6 tries.
